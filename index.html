EmpowerHer Summit 2023:

"Building the level ground for growth"

Join us for an empowering and transformative experience at EmpowerHer Summit 2023! We are thrilled to invite all interested to come together for this remarkable event.

2023.11.03(FRI) ~ 04(SAT)

@ National Theatre, Iganmu, Lagos, Nigeria

Main Program

Lecture

Keynote speeches from influential leaders who have shattered glass ceilings and made a significant impact in their respective fields.

Discussion

Interactive panel discussions where experts will share their experiences, knowledge, and strategies for creating a level playing field.

Workshop

Engaging workshops and breakout sessions that will equip you with actionable skills to navigate challenges and seize opportunities.

Networking

Networking opportunities to connect with a diverse community of individuals passionate about empowering women.

EmpowerHer Allies

participants will be randomly paired up with another attendee, forming powerful alliances to empower and uplift each other. Pairs will engage in meaningful conversations, sharing their personal stories, goals, and challenges.
